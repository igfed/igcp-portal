Additional Login Denied

You are already logged in to this Web server. A new session cannot be created.


Explanation

You have already logged in to this Web server from another client. No more new logins will be permitted until your initial session has ended.




Solutions

  • Resume your original session: If you still have access to your original session, you can return to that client and continue accessing the Web site.
  • End your original session: If you still have access to your original session, you can return to that client and log out from the Web site. Once you have logged out from that client, you can log in from a new client.
  • Wait for your original session to time out: If you no longer have access to your original session (for example, if you closed your browser without properly logging out of the Web site), you can wait for your original session to terminate automatically.
  • Contact support personnel: You can ask your support personnel to terminate your original login session.
